Games I Play

Gorilla Tag

A thrilling VR game where players take on the role of gorillas and use their arms to navigate a virtual world. Whether you're climbing, jumping, or evading, Gorilla Tag offers intense multiplayer fun.

I own a competitive Gorilla Tag team called the Apex Apes. Join us on Discord: Apex Apes Discord

Forza Horizon 5

An open-world racing game set in the vibrant and diverse landscapes of Mexico. Customize your cars, race in dynamic events, and explore breathtaking environments at your own pace.

Forza Horizon 5 is one of my go-to games for relaxing and competitive racing experiences.

Minecraft

The ultimate sandbox game that lets you create, explore, and survive in infinite worlds. From building epic structures to battling mobs, Minecraft offers endless possibilities for creativity and adventure.

I enjoy both survival and creative modes, often collaborating with friends to build amazing projects.

Fortnite

A fast-paced battle royale game combining building mechanics, strategy, and intense action. With frequent updates and new content, Fortnite remains a favorite among players worldwide.

I love teaming up with friends for squad victories or exploring new limited-time events.

Orion Drift

An immersive space exploration game with strategic gameplay and stunning visuals. Pilot your spacecraft, explore unknown galaxies, and forge alliances in this captivating sci-fi adventure.

Orion Drift is perfect for when I want a mix of strategy and exploration in a futuristic setting.
